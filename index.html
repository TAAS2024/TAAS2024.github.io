The International Group for Trustworthy AI and Autonomous Systems

The International Group for Trustworthy AI and Autonomous Systems (TAAS) was co-founded by Associate Professor Zhu Zhongpan from the Shanghai Science Center for Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems and Assistant Professor Wang Cheng from Heriot-Watt University. This groundbreaking international collaboration seeks to advance key technologies and systems in autonomous intelligence, with a special emphasis on smart and green mobility and transportation. Our research focuses on critical areas including autonomous driving behavior modeling, Vision-Language Large Models (VLM) for human-vehicle interaction (HVI), and runtime monitoring for AI safety. Through our joint efforts, we aim to push the boundaries of safe and trustworthy autonomous systems research, ultimately contributing to a safer, smarter, and more sustainable world.
